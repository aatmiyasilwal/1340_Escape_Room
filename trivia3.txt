1. What is the main ingredient in hummus?
a) Chickpeas
b) Tofu
c) Quinoa
d) Lentils

Answer: a) Chickpeas

2. Which country is known for inventing sushi?
a) China
b) Japan
c) Korea
d) Thailand

Answer: b) Japan

3. What is the primary ingredient in guacamole?
a) Avocado
b) Tomato
c) Onion
d) Jalapeno

Answer: a) Avocado

4. What is the Italian dish made of Arborio rice called?
a) Spaghetti Carbonara
b) Fettuccine Alfredo
c) Risotto
d) Lasagna

Answer: c) Risotto

5. Which fruit is also known as the "king of fruits" in Southeast Asia?
a) Pineapple
b) Mango
c) Durian
d) Papaya

Answer: c) Durian
